Known aesthetic risk: fg-faint reads noticeably warmer/goldier than before. The solver preserves hue and saturation in HSL and only moves lightness, but a mid-lightness colour reads more chromatic than a pale one at the same nominal saturation. If that bothers you, the production version should solve in OKLCH and hold perceived chroma constant instead. Everything else is a straight lightness shift.
